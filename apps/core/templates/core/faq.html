Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about shopping locally in Makwanpur.

National platforms dispatch items from central warehouses in Kathmandu or outside provinces, taking 3 to 5 business days. Makwanpur Mart is strictly hyperlocal: all items are stocked by verified vendors right here in Hetauda Wards 1-19. Our local riders deliver within 2 to 4 hours of order confirmation.

Because vendors are independent local shopkeepers, each product displays the exact vendor's return policy right on the product page. If an item arrives damaged or incorrect, you can request an instant replacement at doorstep or initiate a verified dispute via our Grievance Portal.

We believe building digital payment trust takes time. We proudly offer equal choice at checkout: pay securely via NRB-approved gateways (eSewa / Khalti) or pay Cash on Delivery (COD) once you inspect your package from the delivery rider.
Vendor listing is 100% FREE for Year 1! We charge a transparent 5% commission only on successful completed sales. Micro/cottage vendors exempt from individual e-commerce registration can legally operate through our registered DOCSCP platform.
