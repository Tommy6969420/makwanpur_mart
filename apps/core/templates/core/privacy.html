Privacy Policy & Data Encryption

Last updated: July 2026. Explicit data collection disclosure.

Makwanpur Mart prioritizes customer privacy and financial data security above all else.

1. What Data We Collect & Why

  • Mobile Phone Number: Primary identity verification via Sparrow SMS OTP.
  • Delivery Addresses & Ward Numbers: Required to coordinate local Hetauda rider dispatch.
  • Vendor Bank/Payout Details: Encrypted at rest via Fernet field encryption (`django-cryptography`) to process automated earnings payouts.

2. No Raw Payment Storage

We never store credit cards or eSewa/Khalti pin codes. All transactions process via NRB-approved payment gateway tokens.
