Legal Liability Boundary

Makwanpur Mart Vendor Agreement

Under Electronic Commerce Act, 2081 (2025) • Platform Mediator Terms

Important Notice: Makwanpur Mart acts solely as an electronic commerce mediator/intermediary. By signing up, vendors agree that contractual liability for product safety, statutory taxation, and customer dispute resolution rests with the stall seller.

Section 1: Quality & Authenticity

The vendor warrants that all listed items are authentic, non-counterfeit, and accurately described. For cosmetics/beauty items, vendors must guarantee compliance with Nepal Bureau of Standards and Metrology (NBSM).

Section 2: Return & Replacement Obligations

Vendors must publish a clear return window on each product page. If a customer raises a verified defect claim within this window, the vendor must accept the return and provide a replacement or full refund.

Section 3: Commission & Payouts

Makwanpur Mart retains a 5% commission per sale. Net earnings are transferred bi-weekly via NRB-approved bank transfers or eSewa/Khalti accounts.
